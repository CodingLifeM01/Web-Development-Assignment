Racing Miku 2021: Private Ver.


A scale figure of the official character of the 2021 Hatsune Miku GT Project!


The official character of the 2021 Hatsune Miku GT Project, Racing Miku 2021 Ver., is now a 1/7 scale figure! The original 2021 Racing Miku illustration by En Morikura has been carefully brought to life in eye-popping detail. Painted translucent parts are used for her hair, creating a subtly see-through look. Don't miss the dynamic modelling of her hair and outfit, or the vignette-style cobblestone base! Be sure to add this figure of Miku dressed for a private occasion to your collection!
