2023

Nendoroid Snow Miku: Serene Winter Ver.


"Looking up at the winter sky with you."


2023 marks the 14th anniversary of Snow Miku, and the 2023 design was once again selected by fans through online votes between a selection of outfits all submitted to piapro by fans! This year the theme was "Clouds and Skies of the Hokkaido Winter", and the winning Snow Miku design was this version based on illustrations by Isakytm!

The Nendoroid comes with three face plates including a standard face, a smiling face and a singing face. She comes with a miniature figure of Rabbit Yukine, a paintbrush for painting the sky, a Japanese umbrella and a snowflake as optional parts. A special base featuring a sunrise design is included as well. Be sure to add this year's Snow Miku to your collection!
