Hatsune Miku: Symphony 2019 Ver.


"Please listen closely with your heart."


From "Character Vocal Series 01: Hatsune Miku" comes a scale figure of Hatsune Miku based off of the main visual from the full orchestra concert "Hatsune Miku Symphony 2019".

Her pure and delicate dress has been faithfully recreated based on the original illustration by Rella. Her flowing twintails and fluttering skirt have been recreated with the utmost care. Her outfit and hair have been painted with a pearl finish for an elegant appearance. Be sure to add her to your collection!
