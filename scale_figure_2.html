Snow Miku: Snow Princess Ver.


Presenting the lovely snow princess.


The Hokkaido support character Snow Miku is now a 1/7th scale figure! Based on the theme "Princess of the Hokkaido Winter", illustrator KEI key visual of Snow Miku 2019 has been brought to life in outstanding detail. The numerous soft frills of her dress and pearly appearance of her intricate hairstyle are just two highlights of this magnificent figure.

Miku's joyous expression as illustrated by KEI has been faithfully captured and brought to life. A special memorial base featuring a design based on 10 years of Snow Miku history is included. Be sure to add this sweet snow princess to your collection!
