Hatsune Miku: Land of the Eternal


Hatsune Miku from the land of the eternal.


From "Character Vocal Series 01: Hatsune Miku" comes a scale figure of Hatsune Miku based on her outfit from the key visual illustration of the traditional Japanese art, food and music event "KYOTO NIPPON FESTIVAL 2019" which took place at the Kitano Tenmangu Shrine.

The pure and delicate appearance of outfit designer and main visual illustrator Rella's original illustration has been faithfully brought into figure form. Don't miss the intricate sculpting of her twintails and outfit, or the elegant pearl paintwork featured across the figure. Be sure to add her to your collection!
